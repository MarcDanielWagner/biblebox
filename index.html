Why BibleBox?

The Bible has so much to unpack.
The word 'unpack'
invokes the image of a box.
Just as a box can hold many things, the Bible has many things that need explaining or 'unpacking'.

The Purpose.


BibleBox exists to draw out and express biblical knowledge creatively.
It is a place that seeks to creatively express treasures from the bottomless box of the Bible.
